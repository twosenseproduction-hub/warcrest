EXOFRONT

Command the Ashfen Basin

A standalone single-player real-time strategy game. No accounts, no servers.

Choose Your Faction

You lead one faction; the other defends the opposing base.

How to Play

① Harvest

Tap a glowing gold pile — your nearest Pawn mines it and hauls Halcite home automatically.

② Train

Select your Castle to train Pawns, or a Barracks / Archery to build combat units.

③ Build

Select a Pawn, tap Build, choose a structure, then tap a highlighted spot near your base.

④ Command

Tap units to select. Double-tap empty ground to grab your whole army. Tap ground to move, an enemy to attack.

⑤ Attack-Move

Long-press the ground until the ring fills (or arm ⌖ on the rail) to advance while engaging everything.

⑥ Win

Hold off the Cinder waves and destroy the enemy Castle to win. Lose your Castle and it's over.

Drag empty ground to pan · pinch to zoom · thumb rail = zoom, army, stop, attack-move, home · supply caps your army — build Conduits to raise it.
