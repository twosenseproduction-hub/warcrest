How to Play

① Harvest

Tap a glowing Ironstone pile — your nearest worker mines it and hauls ore home automatically.

② Train

Tap your core, foundry, or forge — a ring of options appears around the building. Queue shows as a number badge.

③ Build

Tap the hammer beside the minimap, pick a structure, then tap a valid spot. Your worker hammers until it is done. Tap the core only to train workers and toggle auto-mine.

④ Command

Double-tap empty ground to grab your army (fighters only). Role avatars appear along the bottom — tap one group, then move or attack with just that squad. Double-tap a Pawn to grab all workers.

⑤ Attack-Move

Long-press the ground until the ring fills, or tap ⌖ in the thumb cluster when units are selected.

⑥ Win

Hold off assault waves and destroy the enemy core to win. Lose your own core and it's over.

Double-tap ground = army · tap group avatars to macro · double-tap Pawn = workers · hammer = build.
