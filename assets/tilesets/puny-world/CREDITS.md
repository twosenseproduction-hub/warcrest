# Puny World tileset

- **Pack:** 16x16 Puny World Tileset
- **Author:** Shade (merchant-shade)
- **Source:** https://merchant-shade.itch.io/16x16-puny-world
- **License:** Creative Commons Zero (CC0) — free for commercial use, no attribution required (credit appreciated).

`punyworld-overworld-tileset.png` is the overworld tileset (27 columns, 16x16 tiles)
used as the base art for Warcrest's top-down faction buildings. Per-faction
re-skins (Human stone/blue, Elf wood/green, Orc bone/red) are generated from it
by `tools/art/puny_faction_art.py`.

The companion **16x16 Puny Characters** pack (units, 8-directional, paid) is NOT
included here — see https://merchant-shade.itch.io/16x16-puny-characters-plus-sprites
