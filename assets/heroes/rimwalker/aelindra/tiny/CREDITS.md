# Aelindra hero sprites (Tiny RPG base)

- **Base pack:** Tiny RPG Character Asset Pack v1.03b (20 Characters)
- **Author:** (itch.io asset pack)
- **License:** paid asset — used under the pack's license for this game. Do NOT
  redistribute the raw pack; only Warcrest's customized, game-ready hero sprites
  are included here.

Aelindra Ashveil is the pack's **Wizard** customized for Warcrest:
night-elf lavender skin, leaf-green robe, and tan deer-antlers with leaf buds
(to match her bespoke portrait). Generated by `tools/art/hero_from_tiny.py`.

Side-view strips (100px frames), drawn by `src/hero-sprites.js`, mirrored L/R:
`idle` (6) · `walk` (8) · `attack` (6) · `attack2` (6) · `hurt` (4) · `death` (4).

The original top-down bespoke art lives in the parent folder and is used for her
HUD/loading **portrait**.
